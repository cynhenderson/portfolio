Ultimate Japanese Drugstore Beauty Haul

A comprehensive master list of Japanese equivalents to Shelby Ann's makeup routine, plus absolute J-Beauty holy grails to pick up in Tokyo.

1. Skincare & Prep

Hada Labo Gokujyun Premium Hydrating Lotion

Replaces: Thayers Milky Toner, ELF Drops, Vita Mask Serum

A milky, deeply hydrating toner that is a holy grail in Japan for a plump, hydrated base.

Melano CC Intensive Anti-Spot Essence

Addition: Pre-makeup brightening

A fantastic vitamin C serum for brightening the skin under makeup.

Canmake Mermaid Skin Gel UV (Shade 01 or 02)

Replaces: ELF Sunbeam & Byoma SPF

Acts as a hydrating primer and SPF 50+, giving a glass-skin glow without feeling greasy.

Bioré UV Aqua Rich Watery Essence

Addition: Holy Grail Sunscreen

Lightweight, watery gel that absorbs instantly with zero white cast. Essential for daily protection.

Cezanne Make Keep Base

Replaces: Hard Candy Grip Primer

Highly recommended for keeping makeup locked in all day with a smooth, gripping finish.

Keana Nadeshiko Rice Mask

Addition: Pore-Smoothing Treatment

Sheet masks soaked in 100% Japanese rice extract serum to hydrate and minimize pores.

2. Face Base & Concealer

Tirtir Mask Fit Red Cushion

Replaces: L'Oreal True Match, Essence Stick

The #1 cushion in Japan right now. High coverage, luminous-satin finish, and incredible longevity.

Kate Real Cover Liquid (Light Glow)

Replaces: Revolution Skin Silk Serum Foundation

If you prefer a liquid pump over a cushion, this provides a beautiful, skin-like glow.

Canmake Color Mixing Concealer

Replaces: Catrice Under Eye Brightener

A custom three-shade palette with a peachy shade specifically for neutralizing dark circles.

the SAEM Cover Perfection Tip Concealer

Replaces: Neutrogena Radiant Concealer

Incredibly high coverage but blends flawlessly; perfect for brightening the under-eye.

Excel Silent Cover Concealer

Replaces: Catrice True Skin Concealer

A creamy, three-shade palette ideal for custom-mixing coverage on mature or dry skin.

3. Cheeks, Contour & Highlight

Canmake Cream Cheek

Replaces: SheGlam & Milani Cream Blushes

Melts into the skin beautifully with superior staying power.

Cezanne Natural Cheek N

Replaces: Colourpop Blush

A highly pigmented, finely milled powder blush that rivals high-end formulas.

Canmake Shading Powder

Replaces: Catrice Melted Sun, Wet n Wild Bronzer

A natural, buildable cool-toned powder for sculpting the face (since warm bronzers are rare in Japan).

Kate Designing Eyebrow 3D

Addition: Nose & Subtle Contour

An eyebrow powder palette widely used in Japan for subtle, natural nose contouring.

Cezanne Pearl Glow Highlight

Replaces: Moira Dreamlight

Delivers a blinding, finely milled glow that looks high-end.

4. Powders & Setting Sprays

Canmake Marshmallow Finish Powder

Replaces: Mco Beauty Pressed Powder

A pressed powder that provides a soft-focus, blurring finish that hides pores perfectly.

Chacott For Professionals Finishing Powder

Replaces: L'Oreal Blur Loose & Wet n Wild Powder

A famous stage-makeup brand loose powder known for being sweat-proof and blurring.

Kose Make Keep Mist EX

Replaces: Milani Make It Last, L'Oreal 30sec Mist

A bi-phase formula that locks down makeup beautifully while leaving a natural finish.

5. Eyes & Brows

Excel Skinny Rich Shadow

Replaces: Colourpop & SheGlam Palettes

A buttery, ultra-blendable quad of neutral shimmers and mattes.

Canmake Creamy Touch Liner

Replaces: Eyeshadow used as liner

An ultra-thin (1.5mm) gel liner that glides on without tugging and is completely budge-proof.

Heroine Make Kiss Me Long & Curl Mascara

Replaces: Maybelline Falsies & Essence Lash Princess

Widely considered the best waterproof mascara. Holds a curl all day.

Heavy Rotation Coloring Eyebrow

Replaces: L'Oreal Brow Lamination Gel

The best brow mascara in Japan. Coats every hair and locks them in place all day.

Excel Powder & Pencil Eyebrow EX

Replaces: Colourpop Brow Boss Pencil

A cult-classic 3-in-1 tool (pencil, powder, and spoolie) for creating perfectly defined brows.

6. Lips

Kate Lip Monster

Replaces: L'Oreal Lipstick, NYX Gloss, Revolution Liquid Lip

Applies like a glossy balm, stains the lips, and lasts through eating and drinking.

Chifure Lip Liner

Replaces: NYX & Hard Candy Lip Liners

A creamy, long-lasting drugstore lip liner available in great neutral shades.

Rohto Mentholatum Melty Cream Lip

Replaces: Vitamasques Lip Mask

Melts at body temperature to provide intense, mask-like hydration overnight or during prep.

7. Essential Tools & Removers

&be Black Makeup Sponge

Replaces: Paw Paw Sponge

Designed by a famous makeup artist, it's unbelievably soft for a seamless foundation finish.

Rosy Rosa Jelly Touch Sponges

Replaces: Paw Paw Sponge (Alternative)

A wedge option. Wet these before use; they expand to become incredibly soft.

Shiseido Foundation Brush 131

Replaces: Flat/Buffing Brushes

A multi-award-winning brush that buffs in cream and liquid base products perfectly without streaks.

Rosy Rosa Makeup Brushes

Replaces: Jessie f01 & Jessup Brushes

High-quality synthetic brushes for contour, blush, and eyeshadow at drugstore prices.

Shiseido Eyelash Curler (Japan Version)

Routine Essential

Much cheaper in Japan! Grab the inexpensive replacement rubber pad packs while you're there.

Unicharm Silcot Sponge Touch Cotton Pads

Addition: Tool for Toner

Designed to "give back" liquid to the skin. You use half the amount of toner compared to normal cotton.

Heroine Make Speedy Mascara Remover

Essential Add-on

A must-have for the waterproof mascara. Brush it on, wait 10 seconds, and it melts it right off.
